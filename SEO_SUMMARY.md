## 🚨 Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. ❌ **Don't keyword stuff** - Write naturally for humans
2. ❌ **Don't copy content** - Google penalizes duplicate content
3. ❌ **Don't ignore mobile** - 70% of searches are mobile
4. ❌ **Don't skip alt text** - Important for accessibility & SEO
5. ❌ **Don't use generic meta descriptions** - Make each unique
6. ❌ **Don't forget internal links** - Link related pages together
7. ❌ **Don't ignore page speed** - Slow sites rank lower
8. ❌ **Don't buy backlinks** - Google will penalize you
9. ❌ **Don't ignore Google Search Console** - Check weekly
10. ❌ **Don't give up early** - SEO takes 3-6 months
