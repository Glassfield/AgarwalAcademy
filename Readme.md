---# Agarwal Academy – Home Tuition Platform (React)



\## Website

https://agarwalacademy.in/



---



\## Overview



Agarwal Academy is a \*\*fully responsive, SEO-first React website\*\* designed for providing \*\*home tuition services in South Delhi\*\*, India.



The platform connects \*\*students \& parents\*\* with \*\*verified home tutors\*\* while maintaining tutor privacy, high trust, and scalability.  

All classes are conducted at the \*\*student’s home\*\* — there is no physical coaching center.



The system is built keeping the \*\*Indian education ecosystem\*\* in mind and is future-ready for \*\*AI-powered tutoring features\*\*.



---



\## Core Design Principles



\### 1. Fully Responsive (Mobile-First)

\- Optimized for:

&nbsp; - Mobile phones (primary traffic in India)

&nbsp; - Tablets

&nbsp; - Laptops \& desktops

\- Touch-friendly UI

\- Fast loading on low \& medium bandwidth networks

\- Accessible fonts and spacing for parents \& students



---



\### 2. Modern Color Theme (Tutoring-Friendly)



The UI should use a \*\*clean, trust-building, education-focused color palette\*\* suitable for Indian parents and students.



\#### Recommended Theme

\- \*\*Primary Color:\*\* Deep Blue / Indigo (Trust, Education)

\- \*\*Secondary Color:\*\* Soft Green or Teal (Growth, Learning)

\- \*\*Accent Color:\*\* Saffron / Warm Orange (Indian context, positivity)

\- \*\*Background:\*\* Off-white / Light Grey

\- \*\*Typography:\*\* Clean sans-serif (Poppins, Inter, or Roboto)



❌ Avoid:

\- Dark themes

\- Overly flashy colors

\- Corporate-heavy or aggressive UI styles



---



\## Target Audience



\### Students \& Parents (India-Focused)

\- Classes: \*\*1st to 12th\*\*

\- Boards:

&nbsp; - CBSE

&nbsp; - ICSE

&nbsp; - State Boards

&nbsp; - IB / IGCSE

\- Competitive exams:

&nbsp; - IIT-JEE

&nbsp; - Engineering entrance preparation

\- Languages:

&nbsp; - French

&nbsp; - German

&nbsp; - Russian



---



\## Tutor-as-a-Product Concept



Tutors are presented \*\*like products\*\*, not identifiable individuals.



\### Publicly Visible Tutor Information

\- Display Name (admin-approved)

\- Gender

\- Age Group

\- Subjects \& Classes

\- Area of availability

\- Teaching experience range

\- Avatar (generated from photo)

\- Audio introduction (voice only)



\### Hidden from Public

\- Real name

\- Phone number

\- Exact address

\- Real photo

\- KYC documents



---



\## Tutor Registration (Open to Public)



Tutors can freely register on the website.



\### Tutor Registration Form Includes:

\- Display Name (chosen by tutor)

\- Subjects \& classes

\- Boards \& exam expertise

\- Teaching experience

\- Preferred teaching areas (geo-based)

\- Weekly availability (hours)

\- Indicative charges (per hour)

\- Audio introduction (no real name)



---



\## KYC \& Verification (Admin Only)



Mandatory KYC documents:

\- Aadhaar Card

\- PAN Card

\- Tutor photograph

\- Police verification–related details



✔ Stored securely  

❌ Never shown publicly



---



\## Audio Introduction (High-Trust Feature)



Each tutor records a short audio clip explaining:

\- Teaching style

\- Experience

\- Subjects \& class focus

\- Area they teach in



This helps parents and students become \*\*70–90% confident\*\* before raising an inquiry, without revealing identity.



---



\## Display Name \& Admin Approval Workflow



1\. Tutor submits registration

2\. Admin reviews:

&nbsp;  - Display name

&nbsp;  - Profile content

&nbsp;  - Privacy compliance

3\. Admin can:

&nbsp;  - Approve

&nbsp;  - Edit

&nbsp;  - Reject



Only approved tutors appear on the website.



---



\## Geo-Location Based Matchmaking



\- Students provide their locality

\- Tutors define serviceable areas

\- Matchmaking is done based on:

&nbsp; - Location

&nbsp; - Subject

&nbsp; - Availability



Highly relevant for \*\*Delhi / South Delhi locality-based searches\*\*.



---



\## Admin Dashboard (Non-Technical)



Admin can manage everything \*\*without coding\*\*:

\- Update phone numbers \& content

\- Manage tutor profiles

\- Approve / reject tutors

\- View student inquiries

\- Control visibility



Acts like a lightweight CMS.



---



\## SEO Strategy (India-Focused)



\- Class-wise pages:

&nbsp; - Class 10 Physics Tuition

&nbsp; - Class 12 Maths Tutor

\- Subject-wise pages:

&nbsp; - Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biology

\- Location-based pages:

&nbsp; - South Delhi Home Tutors

&nbsp; - Home Tuition in Delhi

\- FAQ \& blog for Indian parents

\- Schema-ready structure



---



\## Images \& Content Guidelines (Indian Market)



All visuals and content must:

\- Represent \*\*Indian students, parents, and tutors\*\*

\- Use Indian classrooms / home-study visuals

\- Avoid foreign stock imagery

\- Use culturally familiar settings

\- Simple, trustworthy, family-friendly tone



---



\## Future Enhancements



\- AI-powered tutors (concept preview)

\- Smart tutor recommendations

\- Subscription-based tuition packages

\- Demand heatmap by locality

\- WhatsApp integration for inquiries



---



\## Technology Stack (Proposed)



\- Frontend: React.js

\- Backend: API-based (Node.js or similar)

\- Database: Secure cloud DB

\- Storage: Encrypted audio \& document storage

\- Maps: Geo-location APIs

\- Admin: Role-based access control



---



\## Privacy \& Trust Philosophy



Agarwal Academy prioritizes:

\- Tutor safety \& privacy

\- Parent trust

\- Admin-controlled matchmaking

\- No direct tutor exposure
